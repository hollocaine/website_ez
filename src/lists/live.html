EazySAFE are a market leader in Environmental, Health and Safety online training, instructor-led training and consultancy services.

Our comprehensive easy-to-use online library covers environmental, health and safety training courses ranging from employee induction through to workplace stress and much more.

EazySAFE can help your company to;

  • reduce training costs,
  • raise EHS awareness,
  • reduce accidents,
  • improve safety culture and
  • stay in compliance.
We strive to make all aspects of our technology easy to use while also making our course content easy to understand. Our courses are written by our resident environmental, health and safety experts. All our online training courses can be blended with our instructor-led training and tailored to meet your needs.
